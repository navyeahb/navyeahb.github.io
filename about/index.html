About

Download CV

I'm a second-year Economics and Politics student at the University of Edinburgh. I've always wanted to make big, lasting change but I've just never been entirely sure where that change happens. That question has taken me from volunteering with Teach For India, where I first started thinking seriously about education and who gets left out of decisions, to researching infrastructure policy and road safety at Infravision Foundation, to contributing to a published book on workplace culture at Align By Design, to building news tracking systems at NoBroker. Each experience added a different lens. None of them have given me a final answer, which is probably why I'm still constantly exploring.

Right now I'm interested in the overlap between economics, public policy, and education, particularly in South Asia. I'm also exploring law and consulting as career paths, which means I spend a lot of time reading things that don't obviously go together and trying to find the thread.

Outside of academia: I play ultimate frisbee competitively in Edinburgh — I captain my University team — and tennis. I draw mandalas in Procreate (a few of which live on this site), crochet things when I have the patience, and spend a probably unreasonable amount of time listening to music. I'm based between Edinburgh and Pune.
